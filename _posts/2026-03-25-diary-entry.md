> since no one reads what I write, I might as well stop caring and just write for myself

I don’t know what to write,
so I’ll write a poem.
Don’t know which tone to use,
so I’ll try to be solemn.

The world is ending,
but it’s a bit hard to explain.
But my soul still wants to live,
which is hard to contain.

Every job lacks meaning,
doesn’t feel like living a life.
At least writing is still fun,
to be better at it I’ll strive.

I don’t have much to say,
my opinions are not worth sharing.
In some sense that’s a lie,
But today I don’t feel like caring.

I feel more special than anyone,
in the same way I believe everyone is.
My ego and my soul are diverging masters,
I don’t know which one to please.
