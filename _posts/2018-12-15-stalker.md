I still stalk you from time to time

I know you’re happy with him now

and I’m happy for you too

but I still stalk you from time to time

maybe to check if you’re alright

maybe because I like this melancholy

either way,

I can’t help but to stalk you from time to time

darling, I swear

I mean no bad ill

I wish you the best in the world

but some part of me

still believes

that we would be happier together.

I wish I didn’t stalk you from time to time
