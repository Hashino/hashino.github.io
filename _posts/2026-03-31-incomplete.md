> as it will ever be

Moved by desire, I tried to save.
But to do that, I’d need to know what I crave.

For it is not that of what is good that I am after.
It’s knowing of suffering that I need to master.

I wish my pain was the only that I felt.
I wish my grudges were the only I held.

Maybe surrender is the victory attainable.
But for sure to keep fighting is not sustainable.
At least not alone, but in my ranks there are none.
But there’s so little I can do and so much to be done.

I wish to keep on fighting,
but am without weapons to wield.
But to attain them I would need
to lose fights that I will not yield.
